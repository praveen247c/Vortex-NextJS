Product
The platform

The AI Operating System

Vortex IQ detects what's wrong, explains why, and fixes it safely, across every platform a merchant runs.

01

One closed loop, not another dashboard

A continuous detect, diagnose, act, safe-deploy, learn loop. Six modules (Nerve Centre, Vortex Mind, Ask Viq, Actions, Vortex Apps, Vortex Memory) turn raw store signals into resolved work, not more charts.

02

Long-running agents that resolve, not just alert

Agents watch revenue, conversion, inventory, SEO, payments and ops 24/7, surface each issue with its revenue impact, and execute the fix via connected APIs, with one-click rollback and staging guardrails (RollbackPro, StagingPro).

03

The moat: a unified commerce graph + Vortex Memory

Every connector, fix, deploy and decision is written into one live graph across 200+ integrations and 6,000+ KPIs. It compounds with every store, report and question, the part a dashboard-plus-AI competitor can't copy.

04

First-mover on AI-search (GEO)

Beyond classic SEO, Vortex IQ makes catalogues discoverable in AI and conversational search, ahead of the market shift in how buyers find products.

Product
How it works

From insight to action

01

One command centre across every channel

Commerce platforms, analytics, ad channels and ops unify into a single intelligence layer: revenue, spend and risk in real time, not twelve tabs.

02

Findings become done work

AI-generated, AI-ranked findings flow onto a Kanban board with assignees, due dates and automation rules, syncing bidirectionally to Jira, Asana and Linear. The platform defines the work and tracks it to done.

03

One ecosystem, every major platform

BigCommerce, Shopify, Adobe Commerce (Magento) and WooCommerce, managed from one place by merchants and agencies.

04

Agents that act inside the store

Demonstrated agents help create products, landing pages, issue discount codes, run workflows and execute operational actions through platform APIs, every change gated by approval and rollback.
